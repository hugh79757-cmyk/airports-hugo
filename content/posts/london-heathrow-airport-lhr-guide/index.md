## London Heathrow Airport (LHR) Overview

London Heathrow Airport, designated by the IATA code LHR, is located in London, [United Kingdom](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-united-kingdom/) . It serves as one of the primary international gateways to the UK and is situated at coordinates 51.469604, -0.453566. The airport operates within the Europe/London timezone and is known for its extensive connectivity to various destinations around the world.
