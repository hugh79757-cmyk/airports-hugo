## Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) Overview

Charles de Gaulle Airport, known by its IATA code CDG, is located in Paris, [France](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/france-visa-free/) . It serves as one of the primary international gateways to the country and is a significant hub for air travel in [Europe](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-europe/) . The airport is situated at coordinates 49.003197 latitude and 2.567023 longitude, placing it conveniently for access to the capital and surrounding regions. As a major airport, it plays a crucial role in connecting travelers to various destinations around the world.

The airport operates in the Europe/Paris timezone, aligning with the local time throughout the year. Its strategic location makes it an essential point for both business and leisure travelers, facilitating connections to numerous international cities.
