## Direct Destinations from AUH

Abu Dhabi International Airport offers direct flights to one destination: Doha (DOH). This route connects travelers directly to the capital of Qatar, making it a convenient option for those traveling between these two cities. However, the limited number of direct destinations may necessitate connecting flights for travelers looking to reach other locations.
