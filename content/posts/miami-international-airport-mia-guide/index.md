## Miami International Airport (MIA) Overview

Miami International Airport (MIA) is situated in Miami, Florida, and serves as a significant gateway for both domestic and international travelers. The airport operates within the America/New_York timezone and is strategically located at coordinates 25.796, -80.27824. MIA is known for its role as a hub for various airlines, making it a convenient choice for those traveling to and from the southeastern [United States](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-united-states/) .
