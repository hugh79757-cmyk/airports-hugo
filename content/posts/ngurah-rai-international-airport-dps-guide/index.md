Ngurah Rai International Airport, known by its IATA code DPS, is located in Denpasar, [Bali](https://flights.techpawz.com/posts/cheapest-flights-miami-to-bali/), [Indonesia](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-indonesia/). This airport serves as a key gateway to the island, which is renowned for its natural beauty, cultural heritage, and tourist attractions. The airport operates in the [Asia](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-asia/)/Makassar timezone and is positioned at coordinates -8.74446, 115.16331. 

As a significant hub for both domestic and international travel, Ngurah Rai International Airport facilitates a range of flights, catering to travelers from various parts of the world. While the airport may not be the largest in the region, it plays a crucial role in connecting Bali with several major cities across Asia.
