## Ninoy Aquino International Airport (MNL) Overview

Ninoy Aquino International Airport (MNL) serves as the primary gateway to the Philippines, located in Manila. This airport plays a crucial role in connecting travelers to various destinations across [Asia](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-asia/) and beyond. With its coordinates at 14.509602 latitude and 121.01251 longitude, MNL is situated in a region that operates on the Asia/Manila timezone.

As an international airport, MNL is equipped to handle a significant volume of air traffic, facilitating both domestic and international flights. The airport is named after Benigno “Ninoy” Aquino Jr., a prominent figure in Philippine history, and it has become a vital hub for travelers heading to and from the Philippines.
