The airport is situated at coordinates 48.11972 latitude and 16.563583 longitude, placing it conveniently close to the capital city. As a major hub in Central [Europe](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-europe/) , Vienna International Airport plays a significant role in connecting travelers not only to Austria but also to various destinations across Europe and beyond.

The airport operates within the Europe/Vienna timezone, aligning its schedule with local time. While the available data indicates limited airline operations, Vienna International Airport remains an essential gateway for those looking to travel to and from Vienna.
