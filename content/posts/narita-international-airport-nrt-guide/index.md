## Narita International Airport (NRT) Overview

Narita International Airport (NRT) serves as one of the primary international gateways to [Tokyo](https://flights.techpawz.com/posts/cheapest-flights-chicago-to-tokyo/) , [Japan](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/japan-visa-free/) . Located approximately 60 kilometers east of central Tokyo, it plays a crucial role in connecting travelers from around the world to this lively metropolis. The airport operates within the [Asia](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-asia/) /Tokyo timezone and is positioned at coordinates 35.773212, 140.38744.

As a significant hub for international travel, Narita International Airport facilitates a range of flights to various destinations across the globe. It is equipped to handle a substantial volume of passenger traffic, making it an essential point of entry for those visiting Japan.
