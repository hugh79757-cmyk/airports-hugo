## Male International Airport (MLE) Overview
Male International Airport, designated by the IATA code MLE, serves as the primary gateway to the [Maldives](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/maldives-visa-free/). Located in the capital city of Male, the airport plays a crucial role in connecting this island nation to the rest of the world. The airport operates within the Indian/Maldives timezone and is situated at coordinates 4.201389 latitude and 73.524445 longitude.
