## Cascais Municipal Aerodrome (CAT) Overview

This aerodrome serves as a general aviation facility and is situated within the timezone of America/[Nassau](https://tour.techpawz.com/posts/nassau-travel-guide/). The geographical coordinates of the aerodrome are 24.315 latitude and -75.45361 longitude.
