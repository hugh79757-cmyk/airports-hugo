## Sabiha Gokcen International Airport (SAW) Overview

Sabiha Gokcen International Airport, located in Istanbul, Turkiye, serves as one of the two main airports in the city, alongside Istanbul Airport. This airport is situated at coordinates 40.904675 latitude and 29.309189 longitude, placing it on the Asian side of Istanbul. It operates in the Europe/Istanbul timezone, making it convenient for travelers arriving from various parts of the world.

SAW has become an important hub for both domestic and international flights, catering to a wide range of passengers. While the airport is smaller compared to its counterpart, it offers essential services and facilities to ensure a smooth travel experience.
