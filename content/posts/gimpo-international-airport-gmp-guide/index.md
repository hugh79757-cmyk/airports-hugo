## Gimpo International Airport (GMP) Overview

Gimpo International Airport, located in [Seoul](https://flights.techpawz.com/posts/cheapest-flights-miami-to-seoul/) , [South Korea](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/south-korea-visa-free/) , serves as one of the primary airports in the region. With coordinates 37.559288, 126.80351, it is situated conveniently for travelers looking to access the capital city and surrounding areas. The airport operates in the [Asia](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-asia/) /Seoul timezone, making it well-aligned with the local time for both domestic and international travelers.

Gimpo International Airport is known for its focus on regional flights, particularly to destinations within East Asia. It plays a significant role in connecting Seoul to various cities in neighboring countries, as well as domestic locations within South Korea.
