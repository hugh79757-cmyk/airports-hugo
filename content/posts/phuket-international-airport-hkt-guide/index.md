## Phuket International Airport (HKT) Overview

Phuket International Airport (HKT) serves as the primary gateway to the island of Phuket, Thailand. Located at coordinates 8.107619, 98.306435, the airport operates within the Asia/ [Bangkok](https://michelin.techpawz.com/posts/michelin-restaurants-bangkok/) timezone. It is a key hub for travelers seeking to explore the beautiful beaches and attractions that Phuket has to offer. HKT is equipped to handle a variety of international and domestic flights, making it a crucial point of entry for tourists and business travelers alike.
