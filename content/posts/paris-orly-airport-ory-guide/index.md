## [Paris](https://michelin.techpawz.com/posts/michelin-restaurants-paris/) Orly Airport (ORY) Overview

Paris Orly Airport, designated by the IATA code ORY, is one of the two main airports serving the city of Paris, [France](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/france-visa-free/) . Located approximately 13 kilometers south of the city center, the airport is situated at coordinates 48.728283 latitude and 2.3597 longitude. It operates within the [Europe](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-europe/) /Paris timezone, making it a convenient hub for both domestic and international travelers.

Orly Airport is particularly known for its accessibility to various parts of Paris and its role in facilitating air travel to several destinations. While it may not be as large as Charles de Gaulle Airport, it still plays an important role in the region’s air transport network.
