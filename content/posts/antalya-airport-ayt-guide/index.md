## Antalya Airport (AYT) Overview
[Antalya](https://tour.techpawz.com/posts/antalya-travel-guide/) Airport, designated by the IATA code AYT, is located in Antalya, Turkiye. This airport serves as a significant gateway for travelers visiting the region, particularly during the peak tourist season. The airport operates in the [Europe](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-europe/)/[Istanbul](https://flights.techpawz.com/posts/cheapest-flights-chicago-to-istanbul/) timezone and is situated at coordinates 36.89928 latitude and 30.80135 longitude.

## Direct Destinations from AYT
Antalya Airport offers direct flights to four destinations. These include Esenboğa Airport (ESB) in [Ankara](https://tour.techpawz.com/posts/ankara-travel-guide/), Istanbul Airport (IST), Sabiha Gökçen International Airport (SAW) in Istanbul, and Vnukovo International Airport (VKO) in Moscow. The limited number of direct destinations highlights the airport's focus on specific routes, primarily within [Turkey](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/turkey-visa-free/) and a select international location.
